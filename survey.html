Dwarf: A long lived, short and stout race of people known for skill with stone and metal.

Attributes: Constitution +2, 25 speed, dark vision, advantage on saving throws against poison, resistance to poison damage. Proficiency with battleaxe, handaxe, throwing hammer. Proficiency with one of three artisan’s tools: smith’s tools, brewer’s supplies, or mason’s tools. Add proficiency *2 on Intelligence (History) check related to the stonework. Can speak, read, and write Common and Dwarvish.

  • Hill dwarves: Wisdom +1, max hp +1 for every level.
  • Mountain Dwarves: Str +2, Proficiency with light and medium armor.

Barbarian

Barbarians are hand to hand combatants that rely on their strength, their constitution, and their rage in order to fight.

As a barbarian, the following are features of your class at level 1:

Hit Dice: 1d12 per barbarian level. Hit Points at 1st Level: 12 + your Constitution modifier.

Proficiencies: Armor: Light armor, medium armor, shields. Weapons: Simple weapons, martial weapons. Saving Throws: Strength, Constitution.

Rage: In battle, you fight with primal ferocity. On your turn, you can enter a rage as a bonus action. While raging, you gain the following benefits if you aren’twearing heavy armor: You have advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws. When you make a melee weapon attack using Strength, you gain a bonus to the damage roll that increases as you gain levels as a barbarian, as shown in the Rage Damage column of the Barbarian table. You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

Unarmored Defense: While you are not wearing any armor, your Armor Class equals 10 + your Dexterity modifier + your Constitution modifier. You can use a shield and still gain this benefit.

For this class, the following optimizations have been chosen:

Outlander background. Stat array: 15 Strength, 13 Dexterity, 14 Constitution, 10 Intelligence, 12 Wisdom, 8 Charisma. Proficiencies: Animal Handling, Nature, Athletics, Survival, Hand Drum, Sylvan Language. Equipment: Greataxe, 2 handaxes, 4 javelins, a backpack, a bedroll, 2 costumes, 5 candles, 5 days of rations, a waterskin, a disguise kit, staff, a hunting trap, a trophy from an animal you killed a set of traveler’s clothes, and a belt pouch containing 10 gp. Feature: Wanderer - You have an excellent memory for maps and geography, and you can always recall the general layout of terrain, settlements, and other features around you. In addition, you can find food and fresh water for yourself and up to five other people each day, provided that the land offers berries, small game, water,etc.

Bard

Bards are in general are a jack-of-all-trade type of adventurer. Their magic is incorporated with their trade of entertainment, often supportive, and sometimes woven amongst melee attacks.

As a bard, the following are features of your class at level 1:

Hit Dice: 1d8. Hit points at 1st level: 8 + your Constitution modifier.

Proficiencies: Proficiencies Armor: Light armor. Weapons: Simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords. Saving Throws: Dexterity, Charisma

Bardic Inspiration: With words or music you may use a bonus action to inspire one creature within 60 feet and give an additional 1d6 to one of their d20 rolls. That creature gains one Bardic Inspiration die, a d6.

For this class, the following optimizations have been chosen:

Entertainer background. Stat array: 8 Strength, 14 Dexterity, 10 Constitution, 13 Intelligence, 12 Wisdom, 15 Charisma. Proficiencies: Persuasion, History, Deception, Acrobatics, Preformance. Instruments: lyre, lute, flute, hand drum. Tools: Disguise kit. Equipment: A lute, a flute , the favor of an admirer, a costume, and a belt pouch containing 15 gp, a rapier, leather armor, a dagger, a backpack, a bedroll, 2 costumes, 5 candles, 5 days of rations, a waterskin, and a disguise kit. Feature: By Popular Demand - You can always find somewhere to preform, which will usually give you free lodging and food as long as you preform each night, but be careful, you may become a public figure.

Spells: Cantrips- Vicious Mockery, Mending. Spells known- Comprehend Languages, Cure Wounds, Detect Magic, Dissonant Whispers. 2*level 1 spell slot.
