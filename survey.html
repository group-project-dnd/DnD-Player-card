Dwarf: A long lived, short and stout race of people known for skill with stone and metal.
Attributes: Constitution +2, 25 speed, dark vision, advantage on saving throws against poison, resistance to poison damage. Proficiency with battleaxe, handaxe, throwing hammer. Proficiency with one of three artisan’s tools: smith’s tools, brewer’s supplies, or mason’s tools. Add proficiency *2 on Intelligence (History) check related to the stonework. Can speak, read, and write Common and Dwarvish.

    Hill dwarves: Wisdom +1, max hp +1 for every level.
    Mountain Dwarves: Str +2, Proficiency with light and medium armor.

Elf: A long lived race of slender and graceful people known for their affinity with nature.
Attributes: Dexterity+2, 30 speed, dark vision, Proficiency: perception. Proficiency with short bow, shortsword, longsword, longbow. Adv on saving throws vs charmed. Magic can’t put you to sleep, Trance instead of sleep. Can speak, read, and write Common and Elvish.

    High-Elf: Intelligence +1, extra wizard cantrip, extra language. Proficiency with short bow, shortsword, longsword, longbow.
    Wood elf: Wisdom +1, walking speed 35 feet, Can hide when lightly obscured by nature. Proficiency with short bow, shortsword, longsword, longbow.
    Drow: Charisma +1, Darkvision 120 ft. When in direct sunlight, Disadvantage on perception. Proficiency with rapiers, shortswords and hand crossbows. Know Dancing lights cantrip.

Halfling: A short practical peoples that are often kind and curious, known for their comforts of the home.
Attributes: Dexterity +2, size small, speed 25, lucky(reroll your 1 on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws), adv on saving throws vs frightened, can move through spaces of creatures larger than you. Can speak, read, and write Common and Halfling.

Lightfoot: Charisma +1, can be lightly obscured by a creature larger than you.
Stout: Constitution +1, adv on saving throws vs poison, resistance to poison.

Human: Widely adaptable and ambitions, a people who widely vary in tastes, morals and customs. While less long-lived as some of the other races, their ambition spreads them far and wide.
Attributes: all abilities +1, speed 30, can speak, read and write Common and 1 extra language (pre-chosen as dwarvish).

Dragonborn: A tall uncommon race that stands proud, outwardly displaying their draconic heritage by their scales. Known for their drive for self-sufficiency and mastery of individual skills.
Attributes: Strength +2, Charisma +1. Can speak read and write Common and Draconic Ancestory: You have damage resistance and breath weapon as described below.

Blue: Damage type: Lighting. Breath weapon: 5x30 line (dex save)
Brass: Damage type: Fire. Breath weapon: 5x30 line (dex save)
Bronze: Damage type: Lightning. Breath weapon: 5x30 line (dex save)
Copper: Damage type: Acid. Breath weapon: 5x30 line (dex save)
Gold: Damage type: Fire. Breath weapon: 15 foot cone (dex save)
Green: Damage type: Poison. Breath weapon: 15 foot cone (con save)
Red: Damage type: Fire. Breath weapon: 15 foot cone (dex save)
Silver: Damage type: Cold. Breath weapon: 15 foot cone (con save)
White: Damage type: Cold. Breath weapon: 15 foot cone (con save)

Gnome: This race of small folk is known for laughter and enthusiasm for life. Concerned about squeezing the most enjoyment out their centuries of life, they are often known for dynamic expression of their passions and methodical methods.
Attributes: Intelligence +2, size small, speed 25, darkvision, adv on intelligence, wisdom and charisma saving throws vs magic. Can Speak, Read, and Write Common and Gnomish.

Forest: Dexterity +1. Know Minor Illusion Cantrip. Can speak with small beasts.
Rock: Constitution +1. History checks on maic items, lachemical objects, or technological devices + proficiency*2

Half-Elf: Not quite elf nor human, half-elves are usually excellent ambassators, but never totally accepted by either side of their heritage. They retain qualities of both, but live long beyond human lives, often wandering if not fitting in. Often they are more welcome with their human side, but are often afflicted with wanderlust.
Attributes: Charisma +2, 2 other ability scores +1 (pre-determined as wisdom and dexterity). Speed 30, darkvision, adv on saving throws against charmed, and magic cannot put them to sleep. 2 extra proficiencies(Pre-deterimined as Perception and Investigation). Can speak, read, and write, common, elven, and one extra language (predetermined as Sylvan)

Half-Orc: Usually residing among orcs or accepting community of humans, these people stand up to 7 feet tall and their orc-like appearance often puts off others. Known for their likness to their orc lineage, they also possess the traits of their human ancestors, enjoying simple pleasures that fill their hearts with joy.
Attributes: Strength +2, Constitution +1, Speed 30, darkvision, proficiency intimidation. When dropped to 0, once per long rest you can be dropped to 1 hp. When you score a critical hit, you may roll one extra damage dice. Can speak, read, write common and orc.

Tiefling: The children of infernal heritage and humans, they are often suspicious of others, and rightfully so. Their human bretheren liken their horns to their infernal heritage, and treat them as demons, and so are usually self reliant and cautious. Once frienship is gained, usually it is gained for life.
Attributes:Intelligence +1, Charisma+2, speed 30 darkvision, resistance to fire damage, you know thaumatergy cantrip.
