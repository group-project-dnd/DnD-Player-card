Dwarf: A long lived, short and stout race of people known for skill with stone and metal. Attributes: Constitution +2, 25 speed, dark vision, advantage on saving throws against poison, resistance to poison damage. Proficiency with battleaxe, handaxe, throwing hammer. Proficiency with one of three artisan’s tools: smith’s tools, brewer’s supplies, or mason’s tools. Add proficiency *2 on Intelligence (History) check related to the stonework. Can speak, read, and write Common and Dwarvish.

Subraces: Hill dwarves :Wisdom +1, max hp +1 for every level. Mountain Dwarves: Str +2, Proficiency with light and medium armor.
